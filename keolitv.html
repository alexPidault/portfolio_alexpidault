Keoli TV

Keo

Keoli TV is an accurate TV listings app for all the major French channels. The app shows 7 day schedules for over 450 channels and it let user discover more about their favourite shows and movies.

Actually, everything users need to know is include like cast, images, posters, ratings. You can also add an alert on specific program, filter by type of TV show and many other smart features.

App Views
01
TV guide overview

After logging on the app, you discover instantly what's going on TV through channel with real timer bar. You can check out name, hour of ending and the type of any shows. I did choose dark blue for the main background because the app will be mostly use by night.

02
TV show detail

Once you click on a TV show, you access to the resume and even more (cast, picture) by scrolling. In fact, the menu will be fixed on the top and it section will highlighted based on the positon of the page. On the top, there's 3 actions buttons for adding a TV show to your wish list, making an alert or even sharing it to your friends.

03
Description sheet

Whether an actor or presenter, anyyone have a descritptive sheet with further informations about them such as career resume, picture, tv show or filmography they are involed. I put on the top center a picture of the personality and also a background which become blur when user is scrolling down the page.

04
Side menu

All different advanced features are easly accesible by clincking on burger common icon. The side menu background is a light blue to keep a visible separation with main blue of the app. I made icons which are really simple and comprehensible by anyone. You can also find any TV show in search field.

05
Program Overview

With a simple right or left drag, you can easly take a look on every program by hour on any TV channel. By scroolling down, you access to the rest of channel. I decide to make this feature only in portrait mode because of accesibilty. According to me users will pefere to see more tv show in the futur for a specific channel than watch just further channel and only one tv show.
