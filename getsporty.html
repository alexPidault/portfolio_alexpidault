Get sporty

Get

Get sporty is a mobile app availabe on the AppStore that help people to find the right sport class around them without any annual subscribtion.

Filter by whatever sport you want, book your course in advance and invite your friends to participate. You can also rate your coach and let a comment after taking part of the course.

App Views
01
Course detail

The aim was to display all usefull informations availables like resume, a coach picture and his/her rating, course starting hour, gym hall's adress. At the bottom, I design "purchase button" with orange color to focus user on the most probable action to do on this page. There's also friend invit button which is more discret.

Sticky header

By scrolling down the course detail, you can still see what is the type of activity and add this course to your favorite.

Sticky bottom

I've chosen to use sticky buttons visible at the bottom in order to help user to purchase or invite friends to the course without scrolling.

02
Activity explorer

On this page, users can navigate through many types of activity like Danse, Fighting sports, etc .. to make their decision. There's a color palete to differentiate each section blue, red, yellow and green. You can also see directly the price, distance and coach informations without clicking on the course.

03
Search engine

This is a fundamental page when users already knows what kind of activity they want to book. It's really easy to access to your activity by typing the name of it in white search bar. For undecided people, there's a top trends activity searching in past few days.

04
Coach profile

This page help users to learn more about the coach before purchasing their course. That's why I decide to put all useful information as picture, rating and desciription of coach at the top of the page. You can also see what kind of actvity each coachs are teaching by color sport tags.

Card system

All important informations like title and hour course, activity tag, name/rating of the coach are displayed on the right side.

On the top right, you can add the course to your favorite list. Then you can also see the course price tag.

Ticket form

I tried to resume all important informations in the same way than a flight boarding pass. It's well segmented and separate by horizontale/vertical lines.

An easy way to purchase further tickets by clicking + button. Once you made your choice, you click on the purchase orange button.
